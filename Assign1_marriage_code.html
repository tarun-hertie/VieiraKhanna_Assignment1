Analysis of Marriage Trends in the US

About this exercise

This code contains analysis of data on marriage trends in the US from 1960 to 2000. Source data: Decennial Census (years 1960 to 2000) and American Community Survey (years 2001-2012)

Analysis

A preliminary look at the data indicates that the proportion of men and women who never marry has been increasing in the US and has reached almost reached 50%.
